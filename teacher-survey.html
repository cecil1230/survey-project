教师调查问卷

Teacher Survey

Q1. I receive the support they need to strengthen my professional practice.
我获得了加强专业实践所需的支持。
Q2. I use student achievement data to modify and adjust materials and lessons for my students.
我使用学生成绩数据来调整教材和课程以满足学生的需求。
Q3. I have ample resources (material, fiscal and personnel) to assist me in meeting the needs of my students.
我拥有充足的资源（材料、资金和人员）帮我满足学生的需求。
Q4. Leaders regularly evaluate instructional programs and organizational conditions to improve instruction and advance learning.
学校领导会定期评估教学计划和组织状况，以改进教学并促进学习。
Q5. Learners are immersed in an environment that fosters lifelong skills including creativity, curiosity, risk taking, collaboration, and design thinking.
学习者沉浸在一种培养终身技能的环境中，激发创造力、好奇心、勇于尝试、协作和设计思维。
Q6. Learners are provided opportunities to acquire skills needed for their future.
为学习者提供机会以获取未来所需技能。
Q7. Learners are immersed in an environment that promotes and respects student voice and responsibility for their learning.
学习者沉浸在一种鼓励并尊重学生发声和承担学习责任的环境中。
Q8. Learners are provided opportunities to share their opinions about their learning experiences.
为学习者提供就学习体验分享意见的机会。
Q9. Instruction is characterized by high expectations and learner-centered practices.
高期望和以学习者为中心是教学活动的重要特点。
Q10. I am involved in decisions and actions that impact student achievement and the overall functioning of our school.
我会参与影响学生成绩和学校整体运作的决策和行动。
Q11. I am involved in a formal process to verify the success of programs at my school, which are implemented to meet the educational, emotional and personal success of students.
我会参与一项正式流程，以验证学校项目的实施效果，这些项目旨在帮助学生实现教育、情感和个人成功。
Q12. Learner information is used to make decisions about distributing resources.
学习者的信息会用于做出有关资源分配的决策。
Q13. We have used a variety of resources to meet learners' needs and interests.
我们动用了各种资源来满足学习者的需求和兴趣。
Q14. We integrate digital resources that deepen and advance learners' engagement with instruction and stimulate their curiosity.
我们会整合数字资源，加深和促进学习者对教学的参与度并激发他们的好奇心。
Q15. A variety of information for decision-making is used that affected my area of responsibility.
用于决策的各类信息影响了我负责的领域。
Q16. Leaders promote action research by professional staff members to improve their practice and advance learning.
学校领导鼓励专业员工开展行动研究，以改进工作实践并促进持续学习。
Q17. Understanding learners' needs and interests drives the design, delivery, application, and evaluation of professional learning.
了解学习者的需求和兴趣有助于设计、实施、应用和评估专业的学习体验。
Q18. Leaders actively involve me in support of the institution's priorities and guiding principles that promote learners' academic growth and well-being.
学校领导鼓励我参与支持机构的优先事项和指导原则，以促进学习者的学业成长和福祉。
Q19. We base our improvement efforts on learners' needs.
我们的改进工作以学习者的需求为出发点。
Q20. Leaders are focused on continuous improvement.
学校领导专注于持续改进。
