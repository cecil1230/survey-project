家长调查问卷

Parent Survey

Q1. Leaders regularly evaluate instructional programs and organizational conditions to improve instruction and advance learning.
学校领导会定期评估教学计划和组织状况，以优化教学并推动学习进步。
Q2. Learners are immersed in an environment that fosters lifelong skills.
学习者沉浸在一种个培养终身技能的环境中。
Q3. Learners had lessons that prepared them for the future.
学习者接受了为未来做准备的课程。
Q4. Learners are immersed in an environment that promotes and respects student voice and responsibility for their learning.
学习者沉浸在一种鼓励并尊重学生发声并承担学习责任的环境中。
Q5. Learners had opportunities to share their opinions about their learning activities.
学习者有机会分享他们对学习活动的看法。
Q6. Instruction is characterized by high expectations and learner-centered practices.
高期望和以学习者为中心是教学活动的重要特点。
Q7. Learners' needs drive the allocation of resources.
会根据学习者的需求分配教学资源。
Q8. Resources children need for learning are provided.
提供孩子们学习所需的资源。
Q9. Teachers use digital resources to improve learning.
教师会使用数字资源来改善学习体验。
Q10. Teachers use many types of information to help children learn.
教师会使用多种类型的信息来帮助孩子学习。
Q11. They take time to connect with me as a collaborator to improve learners' academic growth.
教师会花时间与我沟通合作，以提高学习者的学术成长。
Q12. They are committed to trying new things to improve the school.
教师致力于尝试新鲜事物以提升学校。
